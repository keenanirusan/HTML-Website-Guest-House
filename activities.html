Things To Do

Hot air balloon safaris

Hot air balloon excursions over Sossusvlei are offered from Sesriem and on the NamibRand Nature Reserve, one of the largest privately-owned game reserves in Africa.

The 4-hour safari includes breakfast and lunch, and a silent glide over this most magnificent of landscapes.

Swakopmund

Swakopmund, known as Swakop in Namibia is the nation’s greatest waterfront town and a resort for Namibians on vacation. The city’s German inceptions are very affirmed in delightful old German Colonial structures all through the city, making a much starker contrast for this town sitting at the edge of the Namib Desert.

  • Art galleries
  • Historical tours
  • Ocean adventures
  • Day spas

See: www.namibian.org/travel/namibia/swakopmund.htm

Dune adventures

Enjoy sandboarding, sand skiing, quad-biking and kite-dune-surfing with qualified, experienced instructors and the best conditions in all of Africa.
