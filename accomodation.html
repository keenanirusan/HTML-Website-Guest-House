Your Home Away From Home

We have 3 individual luxury en suites to choose from namely Dune and Vista located on the ground level and Serenity on the upper level. All suites have their own private entrances and come fully equipped and stylishly furnished for a real "home from home" experience.

Eco-friendly

Namib Guest House take actions to conserve heat, conserve water, install eco-friendly lighting, reduce oil/gas carbon emissions, use renewable natural furnishings, source food locally, offer local employment, conserve as well as care for animals & wildlife. We re-cycle plastic, glass & paper.

We also produce our own home-grown herbs that's organically grown, using no harmful pesticides. Our ingredients are always fresh including free-range farm eggs & milk. Other organic products are locally sourced such as vegetables, fruits, beef, lamb & poultry.
